About

I am a Senior Machine Learning Engineer based out of Hyderabad, currently working for the semiconductor giant, Micron. My work week involves building end-to-end MLOps, mostly on Computer Vision and GenAI, and driving collaboration with multiple teams across geographical borders such as Data Scientists, Data Engineers, Solution Architects, and Stakeholders to achieve robust ML systems that solve real business use cases.

My expertise includes designing and deploying scalable ML pipelines, optimizing large models for low latency, and building streaming data solutions on cloud platforms. I have a proven track record in MLOps, LLMOps, and advanced analytics for manufacturing, as well as experience in Edge AI, cloud migration, and AI product development.

With a strong foundation in Python, Java, R, and C/C++, and hands-on experience with frameworks like PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Kubeflow, I am passionate about delivering impactful AI solutions and sharing knowledge with the community.
